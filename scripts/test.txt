SOXL is the Direxion Daily Semiconductor Bull 3X ETF. 
It is not a normal semiconductor ETF. 
It is a leveraged ETF designed to move about 3× the daily movement 
of the NYSE Semiconductor Index, before fees and expenses. 
Direxion states that SOXL targets +300% daily exposure, 
while its inverse pair, SOXS, targets -300% daily exposure.

As of the latest market data I checked, SOXL was around US$151.89. 
Direxion lists its gross/net expense ratio as 0.91% / 0.75%, 
and the fund started on 11 March 2010.
